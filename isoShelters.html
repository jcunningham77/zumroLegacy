ZUMRO's Rapid Deployed Shelter is now customized to cover multiple emergency functions: Patient Isolation, Hospital Surge Capacity, and additional treatment area with Ante Room when connected to other ZUMRO shelters.

Special Custom Features of the Infection Control Shelter include:

  • Fast and Easy Deployment
  • Built-in 40 sq. ft. Ante-Room (5' Deep by 8' Wide)
  • Multiple Windows with Covers
  • Adjustable Fresh Air Intake Vents
  • Directional Air-Flow Control
  • Available in 216, 284, 311, and 400 Square Feet
  • Can be repositioned after deployment if needed.
  • Built in Zipper Connection on both ends to expand treatment area by
    linking to other ZUMRO Shelters
  • Lightweight and Compact.
  • Effortless Deployment - about 1 minute per 100 sq. ft.
  • Can be positioned and/or repositioned when fully deployed.
