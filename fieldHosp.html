ZUMRO's new model 860 is designed for the growing need of medical surge capacity and other large scale Emergency Shelter needs. Based on our successful framing technology, ZUMRO's model 860 is as strong and as easy to deploy as our popular Model 400. Both ends of the model 860 can be connected to other ZUMRO Shelters including the Isolation Room with integrated Air-Lock Entry and our new Quad Interface Shelter. Various curtain configurations such as patient privacy curtains, room partitions
and center dividers are available. All interior curtains are removable for unobstructed space requirements.
