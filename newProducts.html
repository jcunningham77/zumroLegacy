Interior HVAC Ducting
HVAC Ducting can be easily installed in any ZUMRO Shelter. The Duct System simply “clips” to existing Center Curtain Clips (if so equipped) or clips can be installed in the field to accommodate this new feature. For the larger Shelters the Ducting has dual Heater/AC input connections. The input connections are positioned at opposite ends of the Shelter for added capacity and versatility.
